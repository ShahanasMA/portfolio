What & Why

Sleep is fundamental to health, emotional balance, and cognitive performance — yet many sleep-related disorders like Insomnia, Hypersomnia, and Sleep Apnea go undiagnosed due to the cost and inconvenience of clinical sleep studies and wearable-dependent tracking apps.

SleepShield bridges this gap with a lightweight, hardware-free web application that evaluates behavioral and lifestyle factors — sleep duration, tiredness level, screen time, and interruptions — to assess sleep health without needing any special equipment.

A Random Forest Classifier predicts the user's sleep risk as Low, Moderate, or High, while a rule-based analytical engine analyzes trends across recent logs to flag possible disorder tendencies once enough data is collected.

Beyond prediction, the platform includes trend visualization and a dedicated awareness section, turning SleepShield into a preventive and educational tool rather than just a tracker.

What It Does
Sleep Risk Prediction
Random Forest model classifies each log into Low, Moderate, or High sleep risk based on behavioral features.
Disorder Detection
Rule-based logic flags possible Insomnia, Hypersomnia, or Sleep Apnea from average values across recent logs.
Trend Visualization
Interactive graphs plot sleep duration, interruptions, and tiredness over time to reveal patterns.
Daily Sleep Logging
Simple form to record sleep duration, interruptions, tiredness level, and screen time each day.
Sleep Awareness Section
Standalone educational content on causes, symptoms, and prevention for common sleep disorders.
Secure Authentication
Encrypted login and registration with session management via Flask-Login and bcrypt.
Log History
Users can view, review, and track all previously submitted sleep logs and predictions.
Real-time Results
Risk predictions are returned within 1–2 seconds of submitting a new sleep log.
MySQL-backed Storage
All user credentials, logs, and predictions are securely stored and managed in MySQL.
