// project overview
What & Why

Most learning platforms lock courses to a single stream, charge for the bulk of their content, and rarely push updates — leaving learners juggling several apps just to explore different interests. SkillUp was built to close that gap with one free, unified space for learning.

The app hosts a diverse range of courses across IT, Music, Business, and more, each broken into modules with video lectures, downloadable study notes, and quizzes that reinforce what was just covered.

Two roles drive the platform: Users, who browse, learn, and track their own progress, and an Administrator, who manages active users and uploads or removes course content — study notes, videos, and question papers — keeping the catalog current.

Built with Flutter for a single, cross-platform codebase and backed by Firebase for authentication, storage, and data, SkillUp aims to make learning feel like one continuous, self-paced journey rather than a series of disconnected apps.

What It Does
Multi-Stream Courses
Free courses across IT, Music, Business, and more, all accessible from a single home screen.
Modular Learning
Each course is broken into modules and chapters so learners can progress step by step at their own pace.
Video Lectures
Course-specific videos let learners study concepts visually alongside written material.
Downloadable Study Notes
PDF study notes and question papers can be downloaded straight from the course page for offline access.
Interactive Quizzes
Quizzes at the end of each module check understanding with instant right/wrong feedback.
Progress Tracking
Users can see how far they've progressed through each course and pick up right where they left off.
Trending Courses
The home page surfaces popular courses, helping learners discover new subjects worth exploring.
Admin Content Management
Admins can add or remove study notes, videos, and question papers, and monitor or remove active users.
Dark Mode & Settings
A dedicated profile section offers dark mode, help, and app settings for a personalized experience.
